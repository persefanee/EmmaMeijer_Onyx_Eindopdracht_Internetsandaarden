My personal favorites

Here are some of my personal favorites with a short desctription with each of them. I chose a few art pieces from the last two years, so these pieces aren't that old. Here is my top 3:

  1. Wish for World Peace
  2. Rock'n Roll is Not Dead
  3. Death, Death, Death

1. Wish for World Peace

My first chosen artpiece is called "Wish for World Peace". It was made in the year 2022 and it has these measurements: 100 x 90 x 4.5 cm. Momentarily it is entrusted to Aomori Museum of Art in Japan, Aomori Prefecture.

2. Rock'n Roll is Not Dead

My second chosen artpiece is called "Rock'n Roll is Not Dead". It was made in the year 2022 and it has these measurements: 67.9 x 77.3 x 5.6 cm. It was made with acrylic and grease stick on wood.

3. Death, Death, Death

My third and last chosen artpiece is called "Death, Death, Death". It was made in the year 2023 and the size is not confirmed yet. It was painted on pieces of corrugated board mounted on wood with revised technique and material.
